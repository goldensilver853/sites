Changelog for Monoblocks 11.0:

  • New creative tabs
  • Blocks and items have been more strictly organised into the creative tabs
  • Normal blocks go into the 'Monoblocks' tab
  • Signage and traffic items go into the 'Monoblocks Signage' tab
  • Anything that emits light (with exceptions) goes into the 'Monoblocks Lighting' tab
  • Items will continue to go into the 'Monoblocks Items' tab
  • Added Stucco and Vinyl Siding blocks
  • Changed version numbering system

Monoblocks is a fun mod which includes many blocks for creative and survival builders alike. You can craft dyed versions of many blocks including wood planks, stone, stone bricks, iron, cobblestone and glowstone. You can use plain colored blocks if you looking to build something simple. The mod adds lots of fencing options too so now your army of sheep, pigs and cows will no longer escape from their pens. Monoblocks also adds blocks that include addtional functions such as smelting with the Iron Furnace, storing items with the stone chest, lighting your land with numerous lighting options and listening to your favorite music with the stereo. The mod also adds weaponry and tools such as lightsabers, pickaxes, shovels, hoes, axes and a long range ammunition propelling device. If you ever happen to get hungry, don't fear because many food items are avalible with Monoblocks. Sink your teeth into a pizza, donut, fries or if you are really hungry, a burger pattie. Monoblocks is also great for modern map makers with its additions of road related blocks including numerous traffic signs, several traffic lights and a few street lights. Monoblocks also has translations for several languages, including Chinese. The possibilities are endless with Monoblocks.
